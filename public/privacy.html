Privacy Policy

What We Collect

VoteyMcVoteFace collects information that is sent directly to it, via direct message or when the bot is directly mentioned or in a channel.

VoteyMcVoteFace also collects Slack account and access information from users for the purposes of connecting to the Slack API and to authenticate access to information on the VoteyMcVoteFace website.

Using standard web analytics and web server technologies, VoteyMcVoteFace logs your navigation actions, IP address, cookies and other information provided by your web browser.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to learn about the applications we run, and to improve the applications we create.
